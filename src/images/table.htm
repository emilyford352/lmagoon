        WKXL 103.9-Concord News Radio

        These are the days that almost make us hang up our mics. The days when there are no answers, and nothing makes sense anymore, but somehow we need to fill air a...nd talk. However, in times like this, when so many are hurting, this is when we dig deep and stick to our local roots. We can't solve mass killings, and we can't make sense of mental health issues, but we can celebrate life, good things happening in our backyard, the everyday acts of community here in Concord. We can also choose to use our airwaves to call to order discussions on mental health needs, money needed for awareness, and we can host discussions to keep our politicians accountable.
        Call us naive but this is a choice and that's where we choose to focus our energy. We refuse to sensationalize anything else. It's why we never report from chatter on a police scanner and why we won't post mug shots on this site. Life might not be all roses but making a choice to promote the good is what our recent owner has committed his station to and we think you'll have a better listening experience because of it. www.concordnewsradio.com #alwayslocal #aslowerpace #newsyouneedtoknow #itsachoice
